Sri Venkateswara University, [SVU] Tirupati Hostels and Facilities

Facilities:
 Rooms:
  • Shri Venkateshwara University provides immaculate hostel rooms for both men and women.
  • Separate hostel facility has been created for men and women keeping in view the need of discipline, security and comfort.
  • A high level of hygiene is observed in the campus.
  • We strive to provide the best facilities to hostellers.
  • Uninterrupted power and water supply is in the hostel rooms.
  • 24 hours security is deployed on the campus to ensure the safety of the students.
  • High standard of discipline is also ensured on the campus. Hot water is supplied during the winter.
  • Water coolers are attached with R.O. installed on each floor.
  • Double, Three and Four students are accommodated in a room depending on its size.
  • Common rooms in every hostel are equipped with modern recreational activity equipments.
 Library:
      The University Library, centrally situated and easily accessible to all the departments on the campus has steadily grown over the years and it has 3,68,315 documents as on 31st March 2014. The collection of the library includes textbooks, reference books, general books, gift books, reports, back volumes of journals, M.Phil., & Ph.D., dissertations and e-documents The library has four reading halls with a seating capacity for 400 users at a time.
 Bank:
     The University has banking facilities with ATM on its campus, besides a regular Post Office. The campus has a modern telecom centre that provides telephone, fax, e-mail and telegraphic services.
 Canteen:
     Cafes at convenient locations in the campus cater to the tastes of the inmates and visitors alike. The canteen is kept clean and maintained by a separate group of staff.
 Internet:
     There is an internet hub inside the campus which is equipped with ten computers. The hub is open from 7.00 A.M. to 9.00 A.M. and 4.00 P.M.to 10.00 P. M. on all days except Sundays. The students can utilize this facility out of class hours.
 Gymnasium:
     The University has a sophisticated infrastructure to promote an athletic spirit among its students. The Gymnasium has one Indoor Floodlit Stadium with wooden flooring for Badminton and gymnasium for its Physical fitness programme. It also has one Indoor Recreation hall with the facility to play Table Tennis, Chess and Carrom. It also has one exclusive Hockey field, Two Flood lit Tennis courts a Basketball court with galleries , Two Volleyball courts & Two Badminton courts.
